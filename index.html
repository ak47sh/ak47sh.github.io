How it Works
We are a business that partners with other companies to invest in their products and share the profits with our customers. We first partner with other businesses that sell products or services that we believe in. When a customer purchases something from one of our partner businesses, we receive a percentage of the profits. We take the profits we earn from our partnerships and invest them in ways which deliver strong returns. Once we earn a return on our investments, we share the profits with our customers. At Kasu, we're committed to creating a business that benefits everyone involved.
